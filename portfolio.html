Project #1: Build A Snowman


This snowman program allows users to build their snowman however they want. They may add features and accessories to the snowman or not build one.

Project #2: Nimble Knights

This program allows users to control a knight and attack other players (which are programmed to be easy). Once the player has died, they can see their score and place on the leaderboard.

Project #3: Make an E-Card Invite for Halloween Event


When the user runs this program, they can put down who the card is to and who it is from. Then, they can either choose to make their own custom message or use the premade message for the card. There is even a little cute interaction with a pumpkin and a ghost before they get to the card.
